🎭 Culture of Chittoor

🎶 Music & Dance

Chittoor is deeply rooted in classical traditions like Kuchipudi and Bharatanatyam, along with folk dances performed during village festivals and jatras.

🕌 Temples & Traditions

The region is famous for temples like Tirupati, Kanipakam, and Sri Kalahasti. Rituals, pujas, and annual temple fairs play a major role in community life.

🍛 Food & Cuisine

Chittoor's cuisine is known for spicy Andhra delicacies such as pulusu, pachadis, dosas, and biryani, often enjoyed with local festivals and celebrations.

🎨 Handicrafts & Art

Local artisans create wooden carvings, traditional rangolis (muggulu), and crafts inspired by temple culture, reflecting Chittoor’s artistic heritage.
