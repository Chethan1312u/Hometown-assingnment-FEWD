🌏 Places to Visit in Chittoor

Home Festivals Culture Economy Things to Do

⛩ Tirumala Venkateswara Temple

One of the worlds most visited pilgrimage sites, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara. Millions of devotees visit every year, making it the spiritual heart of Chittoor.

🙏 Kanipakam Vinayaka Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, known for its miraculous self-manifested idol that is said to grow in size every year.

🕉 Sri Kalahasti Temple

Called the "Kashi of the South", this temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is famous for Rahu-Ketu poojas.

🏯 Govindaraja Swamy Temple

A historic temple in Tirupati with massive gopurams, reflecting Dravidian architecture and spiritual heritage.

💧 Kapila Theertham

A Shiva temple at the base of Tirumala hills with a picturesque waterfall flowing right next to it.

🌊 Talakona Waterfalls

The tallest waterfall in Andhra Pradesh, located in Sri Venkateswara National Park. A paradise for nature lovers and trekkers.

🏞 Kailasakona Waterfalls

A serene waterfall near Nagalapuram, associated with Lord Shiva. Known for its scenic beauty and peaceful surroundings.

💦 Kaigal Waterfalls (Dumukurallu)

A beautiful forest waterfall with clear streams, perfect for eco-tourism and village trips.

⚔ Chandragiri Fort

A historic fort from the 11th century, linked to the Vijayanagara empire. Features royal palaces and stunning hilltop views.

⛰ Nagari Hills

Famous for trekking, adventure, and its iconic Nagari Nose rock formation with panoramic views.

🌄 Horsley Hills

A cool hill station with panoramic views, adventure sports, and a peaceful climate. A popular weekend getaway.

🪨 Silathoranam (Natural Arch)

A unique natural rock arch formation near Tirumala, considered sacred and geologically rare.

🦁 Sri Venkateswara Zoological Park

One of the largest zoos in Asia, home to tigers, lions, and rare wildlife. A fun and educational family spot.
