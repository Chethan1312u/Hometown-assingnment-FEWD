Festival Highlights

Chittoor is not only known for its scenic beauty and temples but also for its rich cultural celebrations. Festivals here are celebrated with great enthusiasm, devotion, and community gatherings. From traditional Hindu festivals to famous jatara events, each occasion reflects the soul of Chittoor.
