🌾 Agriculture

Chittoor is known as the "Mango District" of Andhra Pradesh. It produces mangoes, groundnuts, sugarcane, and paddy as major crops.

🏭 Industries

The district has industries in dairy (Tirumala Milk), sugar, granite, mango pulp processing, and small-scale manufacturing units.

🚚 Trade & Exports

Chittoor exports mango pulp, granite, dairy products, and textiles to national and international markets.

💼 Employment

Most people are employed in agriculture, followed by industries, government jobs, and temple administration in Tirupati.
