🎉 Things to Do in Chittoor

Home Festivals Culture Economy Places to Visit

🙏 Seek Blessings at Tirumala

Visit the world-famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple and experience the spiritual atmosphere with millions of devotees.

🥾 Trekking at Talakona & Horsley Hills

Explore scenic trekking trails at Talakona waterfalls and the cool climate of Horsley Hills for an adventure in nature.

🌊 Picnic at Waterfalls

Relax with friends and family at Kailasakona and Kaigal waterfalls. Ideal spots for day trips with beautiful landscapes.

🏰 Explore Historical Chandragiri Fort

Walk through the ruins of Chandragiri Fort, a landmark of Vijayanagara history, and learn about its royal past.

🚴 Adventure Sports

Try zorbing, rock climbing, and cycling in Horsley Hills, a perfect getaway for thrill seekers.

🪨 Visit Natural Wonders

See the natural rock arch Silathoranam near Tirumala, a rare geological formation with cultural significance.

🦁 Visit Sri Venkateswara Zoological Park

Spend time with wildlife at one of Asia’s largest zoos, home to tigers, lions, and exotic birds.

🎪 Attend Local Jatharas

Join colorful village fairs and jatharas like Kanipakam Brahmotsavam to experience Chittoor’s vibrant culture and traditions.
